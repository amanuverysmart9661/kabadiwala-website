Our Initiatives

Tetra Pak Initiative

UBC collection & reuse

In partnership with Tetra Pak India, we collect Used Beverage Cartons (UBCs) from homes, schools and retail outlets. Collected cartons are routed to certified recyclers and converted into building materials, stationery and insulated panels — diverting cartons from landfill and saving trees.

Collections in 80+ cities
School drives & pick-ups

Mattress Circular Journey

Feko Nahi, Recycle Karo

Our large-scale mattress recycling dismantles old mattresses into coir, foam, fabric and metal, which are then reintroduced into manufacturing. This reduces bulky-waste in landfills and creates supply for local recycling industries. How you benefit: safe collection, zero-hassle pickup, and reduced disposal fees for large items.

Bulk pickup available
Workshops for societies

Tree Plantation

Reforesting communities

For every ton of material recycled via Aone Kabadi, we contribute to plantation drives with partner NGOs and local communities. To date we’ve supported 10,000+ saplings, created green corridors and engaged volunteers in neighbourhood drives — turning waste into living assets.

10,000+ saplings planted
Community volunteers

Kitab Ghar

Books for every child

We collect gently-used books and distribute them to community libraries and schools in underserved areas. The program reduces paper waste and boosts literacy — 50,000+ books donated so far. Donate old textbooks or request books for a community library near you.

50,000+ books donated
Community libraries

E-Waste Drive

Responsible device recycling

Collection events for old phones, chargers and household electronics. We partner with certified e-waste recyclers to ensure data-safe, environmentally-sound disposal and material recovery. Safe drop-off points and scheduled pick-ups available for bulk collections.

Data-wipe & recycle
Certified partners

Plastic Circular Drive

Turning waste into raw material

Community collection drives for PET and HDPE plastics; materials are cleaned and processed by partner units into pellets for reuse in packaging and industrial products. Participants earn credits for plastic turns which can be redeemed against pickups or merchant offers.

Credit rewards
Pelletising partners
